About Us

We are a rapidly growing EdTech Company that assists students in searching and applying to overseas universities. We have overseas education assistance to 700+ global universities and institutions across 29 countries in the world such as USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, Singapore, Europe and many more.

Our industry experience spans over two decades - fulfilling dreams of study abroad aspirants, building strong and credible relationships with our stakeholders including our Partner Institutions and Business Associates. Our expertise lies in helping students in making informed decisions by choosing the country, university and program suiting their preferences and career aspirations. We offer best-in-class services to students for applications and visa processing, provide coaching for English Proficiency Tests such as IELTS, TOEFL (iBT) & PTE. We offer end-to-end assistance for Overseas Education Loans, which is our distinct offering for study abroad aspirants.
